Instructions on how to get the result

  1. Enter Target and Query sequences
  2. Click Submit
  3. Then an alert pop will appear confirming submission
  4. Click on "Back" and you will redirected back to homepage
  5. Click Get Result and then view your result


NOTE: Your sequences are stored on the server. Use Delete button to delete them.
